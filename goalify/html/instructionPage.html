Welcome to Goalify!

Track your daily goals with a simple layout, live stats, and a responsive calendar.

Here's how everything works:

Creating Goals

Click the "Create Goal" button.

Type your goal and press enter. It will then create your personalised daily goal.

Completing & Deleting Goals

Check a goal off to mark it complete.

To delete a goal, click to select one or more (highlighted in red), then click "Delete Goal".

Daily Reset & Calendar

Every midnight, all goal checkboxes reset automatically.

The calendar updates based on your goal completion:

Today (in progress)

All goals completed

Incomplete goals

Saving Your Data

Your goals are saved locally using localStorage.

Your data is private and never uploaded.
